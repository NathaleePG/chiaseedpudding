Chocolate Chia Seed Pudding

Vegan, Dairy Free, Gluten Free

Craving for something sweet? Check out this yummy recipe thatâ€™s perfect for breakfast on-the-go or as a snack! This recipe is high in omega-3 fats which are vital for our cognitive health, and it is also great fibre and antioxidants!

Ingredients:
(2 servings)
6 tbsps of gluten free chia seeds (black or white or both)
1/2 cup of non dairy vanilla yogurt (soy or coconut)
1 tsp of gluten free flax seeds
1 cup of almond milk
1-2 tbsp of maple syrup
1 & 1/2 tbsp gluten free cacao powder
A handful of your favourite berries

Things you need:
Large mason jar or glass
Measuring spoons and cups
Spoon

    Instructions:

  1. In a mason jar or glass, add the chia seeds, flax seeds seeds, gluten free cacao powder, and maple syrup. Adding these in first will prevent you from spilling the recipe while you mix.
  2. Next add in the yogurt and almond milk and stir until everything is fully incorporated.
  3. Let the pudding chill in the fridge for about 4-5 hours or overnight. The chia seeds will double in volume and create that pudding like consistency.
  4. Your batter should make 6 pancakes (2 and a half tablespoons each)
    5. Once the chia seeds have doubled in size, add in your favourite berries (I prefer strawberries and blueberries for those healthy antioxidants!).
